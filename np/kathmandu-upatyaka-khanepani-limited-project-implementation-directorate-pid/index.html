Description
Kathmandu UpatyakaKhanepani Limited (KUKL) is distributing drinking water in the valley in limited quantity by utilizing available ground and surface water resources. However, water supply and wastewater management in Kathmandu Valley has become a challenging concern owing to the inadequate sources water sources coupled with poor distribution pipe network. As the valley population is increasing over the years, scarcity of clean and safe drinking water is hugely felt. At present, KUKL, under a License and Lease Agreement with the Kathmandu Valley Water Supply Management Board (KVWSMB) is involved in operating water supply and waste water services in the valley. On the other hand, Project Implementation Directorate (PID) is formed under KUKL Board to set up essential infrastructure for distributing drinking water to each household of Kathmandu on a regular basis. PID has been working on immediate improvement works in the existing water distribution system, improvement of treatment plants, construction of service reservoirs, Distribution Network Improvement (DNI) on the basis of District Metering Area (DMA), construction of Bulk Distribution System (BDS) and improvement of waste water management system in the Valley.
